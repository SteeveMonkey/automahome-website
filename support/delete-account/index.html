Deleting your account

Once you delete your AutomaHome account, all of your associated data is deleted as well. This data includes your login credentials and name. This will also delete any homes you own, so any users that have been invited to a home that you are the owner of will lose access once you delete your account.

Open the app

First, open the app and ensure that the account you are trying to delete is currently logged in. If not, then sign out of the current account and sign into the one that you want to delete.

Navigate to account settings

Open the navigation drawer by tapping the hamburger menu at the top of the screen, then tap "Settings" near the bottom of the drawer, and then tap the "Account" tab located near the top of the screen.

Delete the account

Press the red "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the screen and confirm to confirm that you are actually trying to delete your account and all of it's associated data.
